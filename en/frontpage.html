Noah's Ark is a non-denominational Christian organisation in Uganda that focuses on helping the most vulnerable children in society and the poverty stricken community to benefit the immediate area and the country as a whole.

Children's Home

Our Children's Home gives shelter to children who were abandoned, abused or unwanted by their families. Some were literally thrown away with the rubbish. At Noah's Ark they receive the love, care and attention they deserve because they are somebodies.

Schools

More than 350 students from the home and community attend our schools through our sponsorship program. They are provided with a future-proof education based on the Ugandan National Curriculum supplemented with extra activities and vocational skills.

Medical Clinic

Our Medical Center offers quality and affordable health care to the children, staff and the surrounding community. We conduct outreaches to remote communities and schools for sensitizations, screenings and immunizations.

How You Can Help

All of this would not be possible without help from you! Find out how you can help us by sponsoring a child, sponsoring an education, volunteering at the home or by making a donation.
