Family Clinic

The Noah’s Ark Family Clinic was opened in 2009 and provides medical care to all those who live within Noah’s Ark. It also extends this service to the people that live in the surrounding area. The family Clinic is registered as a health center, which means that minor injuries can be dealt with and common diseases like malaria, typhoid and flu can be treated. The clinic has its own delivery room, pharmacy, medical ward, malnutrition unit, Dental unit and laboratory. There is an ambulance on standby for emergencies. The process for treatment, testing and medicine are purposely kept low to allow more people to seek medical attention and care. The medical staff that live on the compound are available 24/7 and include a doctor, nurses, midwives and a lab technician.

Location We are located along Mukono-Kayunga (Bwgerere Road), about 5km north of Mukono Town. Branch off Kayunga Road at the signpost for "Seeta High Green Campus" and continue down the road. Access to the clinic is via the main gate of Noah's Ark Children's Ministry on your right.
