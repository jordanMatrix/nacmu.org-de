Join us and make a difference to the lives of others

One of the most lasting ways you can make a difference in the lives of the children and staff is to visit us at Noah's Ark and Volunteer your time. Throughout the whole year we have volunteers from around the world staying with us. And not only young volunteers, some have even been 70 years old.

We ask that volunteers come for a minimum of three months. This way you're able to make a lasting impact on Noah's Ark and the lives of everyone here at the home. It will also give you time to get to know the children and be part of every day life here at Noah's Ark. We are looking for volunteers that ara at least 25 years old, have completed your studies in a relevant area and have a positive Christian atitude.

We're regularly looking for volunteers who would like to:

  • Teach
  • Help with IT set up
  • Social Media
  • Marketing
  • Fundraising
  • Engineering
  • Office and Admin support
  • Teach musical instruments
Or if you have a specific project you'd like to help with, please let us know and we can see what's available.

We ask that all volunteers be independent self-starters, results oriented and able to get tasks done without much direction. We have set times for volunteers to come:

  • February - April
  • May - July
  • August - October
  • November - December
